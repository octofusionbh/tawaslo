## 3. Competitive gaps → prioritized build list

### 🟢 Quick wins (high impact, low effort)

1. **AI "best time to post" auto-scheduler.** All of Buffer/Hootsuite/Sprout push this and report **25–40% engagement lift**. We already compute a best-time heatmap in Analytics — turn it into a one-click "schedule at best time" in the composer/planner. *Our angle: tuned to GCC audience activity + prayer/weekend rhythms.*
2. **AI report narrative.** Sprout turns raw data into "executive-ready insights." Add an AI summary paragraph at the top of our Reports/PDF ("This month reach grew X%, Reels drove most engagement, do more of Y").
3. **"Repurpose top post" + "Post from a URL/keyword."** Hootsuite's OwlyWriter headline features. We have caption gen — add two buttons: paste a URL/keyword → full post, and recycle a top-performing past post.
4. **AI sentiment + reply suggestions in the Inbox.** We already built sentiment for Streams and AI replies in Inbox — surface a sentiment dot + auto-suggested reply on every message like Sprout's AI Assist.

### 🟡 Medium bets

5. **Full white-label client portal.** SocialPilot/Sendible sell this hard. We have the portal — add custom logo + custom domain/subdomain + "remove Tawaslo branding" toggle for Enterprise. (Big agency upsell.)
6. **Bulk CSV scheduling at scale (up to ~500 posts).** SocialPilot's headline. We have CSV import — verify it scales and add a bulk preview/queue.
7. **Review management beyond Google.** Vista Social covers 17+ review sites. Extend our Business Profile to also pull TripAdvisor/Zomato/Talabat-style reviews relevant to GCC F&B clients.
8. **Ads management depth.** Metricool bundles ad management. We have an Ads page + Meta Ads MCP — make it create/boost/report campaigns properly.

### 🔵 Big bets (differentiators / later)

9. **DM automation (keyword triggers → auto-replies + routing).** Vista Social's standout. Gated on Meta messaging approval, but huge once live — especially WhatsApp auto-replies for GCC businesses.
10. **AI "social intelligence" layer (Trellis-style).** Sprout's premium tier: cross-account insights, anomaly alerts ("your competitor just went viral"), content recommendations. We have the data (trends + competitor + analytics) — wrap an AI advisor over it.
11. **Pinterest / Threads / X depth** — fill remaining network gaps if clients ask.

## 4. Where to double down (our moat — don't lose it)

- **Bilingual AR/EN + Khaleeji/Saudi/Egyptian dialect** content — keep it best-in-class. No global tool competes here.
- **GCC-native**: local trends, Google Business, WhatsApp, Benefit/local payments, prayer/weekend-aware scheduling.
- **No-CR, local-priced billing** via Paddle — lower barrier than enterprise tools.
- **Reel/Video Studio** — keep it a flagship; nobody else writes shoot-ready scripts.

---

## 5. Suggested order

1. Finish **Paddle** (revenue is the priority).
2. **Quick wins #1–4** (best-time scheduler, AI report summary, repurpose/from-URL, Inbox sentiment) — small effort, directly match the majors' headline AI features.
3. **Kill remaining fake stats** + landing price sync (trust/consistency before launch).
4. **White-label portal** (#5) — the clearest Enterprise upsell.
5. Then big bets as Meta approvals land.
