# Tawaslo Launch Teaser, Instagram Content Calendar

A three week "coming soon" campaign that builds anticipation, shows the product in snippets, and drives waitlist signups before launch. Bilingual throughout (English and Arabic), because that is the whole point of Tawaslo.

## Campaign at a glance

Goal: awareness plus waitlist signups before launch day.
Handle: @tawaslo
Duration: three weeks. Week one teases, week two reveals features, week three counts down and launches.
Cadence: three feed posts a week (Sun, Tue, Thu) plus light Stories on the in between days.
Primary hashtags: #Tawaslo #ComingSoon #SocialMediaManagement #بالعربي #تواصلو #GCCstartups
Tone: confident, modern, regional pride. Helpful, never salesy.
Visual identity: slate and steel (#6E8CAB, #4F6B8C), clean white space, large serif numerals for any figures, real product screens where possible. Calm and premium, not loud.
Call to action across the campaign: "Join the waitlist, link in bio."

A note on captions: emojis are welcome here, this is social content, not the product UI. Each post below has an English and an Arabic caption ready to paste.
